Frequently Asked Questions

Where does the data come from?

All appointment data is sourced directly from the official Polish National Health Fund (NFZ) website at terminyleczenia.nfz.gov.pl. The data is updated regularly by NFZ.

How current is the data?

The app downloads the latest data from NFZ servers. You can see the data date in the results screen. Pull down to refresh and get the newest data.

Why can't I find a specific facility?

Only facilities registered with NFZ and accepting public health insurance patients are listed. Private-only facilities are not included in the NFZ database.

How do I book an appointment?

The app shows available appointments and contact information. To book, call the facility directly using the phone button in the app.
