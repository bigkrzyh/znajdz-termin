Introduction

Znajdź Termin ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

1. Location Data

With your permission, we access your device's approximate location to:

  • Suggest your voivodeship based on your current location
  • Calculate distances to medical facilities
  • Provide navigation to selected facilities

Data handling: Location data is processed locally on your device and is NOT transmitted to our servers. We do not store your location history.

2. App Usage Data

We store your preferences locally on your device, including:

  • Last selected voivodeship
  • Cached medical facility data for faster loading

Data handling: This data never leaves your device.

Advertising and App Tracking Transparency

This app displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob.

Tracking Permission

When you first open the app, you will see Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt asking for permission to track your activity. This is required by Apple for apps that use advertising.

  • If you allow tracking: You may see personalized ads based on your interests
  • If you deny tracking: You will see non-personalized ads. The app works the same way.

Data Collected for Advertising

When you use the app, Google AdMob may collect:

  • Device identifiers (advertising ID - only if you allow tracking)
  • Usage data (ad interactions, app usage patterns)
  • Approximate location (IP-based)

How to Change Your Choice

You can change your tracking preference at any time:

  • iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → Find "Znajdź Termin" and toggle on/off
  • iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising → Disable "Personalized Ads"

For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone)
  • Health or medical records
  • Account or login information
  • Contacts or address book
  • Photos or media
  • Financial information

Data Retention

All locally stored data (preferences, cache) can be deleted by uninstalling the app. We do not retain any data on external servers.

Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The app content (medical appointments) is intended for adults.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect the data stored on your device. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Deny location access at any time in device settings
  • Opt out of personalized advertising
  • Delete all app data by uninstalling the app
  • Request information about data processing
